PicoGram

PicoGram is a lightweight photo sharing web-application modeled after Instagram. PicoGram uses PostgreSQL for its database, Ruby on Rails for its backend, and React.js with a Redux architectural framework on the frontend. It features photo upload, photo feed, photo like, photo comment, and user follow functionality.
